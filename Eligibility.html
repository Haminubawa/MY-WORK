A Candidate must meet the following criteria in order to apply for this scholarship

  • Must be a Nigerian
  • Must have a good academic performance
  • Must be sound morally and academic
  • Must be residing in Nigeria
  • Beign a Kano state Indigene is an added advantage

Any candidate applying for Bright Future Academy must possess

  • International Passport
  • 9 credits in WAEC/NECO
  • A Bachelors Degree for MSc Scholarship
  • A Masters Degreee for PhD Scholarship
  • A good CGPA not less than 3.5/5.0 or 7.0/10

Bright Future Foundation provides the following funds

  • Accomodation
  • Feeding
  • Monthly Stipends
  • Materials and Reagents
  • Hazard Allowance
